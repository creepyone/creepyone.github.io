About Project

Introduction

We bring to your attention a new software product for extracting the root of the second degree (square root) from real non-negative number!

We wish you will have a great time with this program, get a lot of positive experience while using "SQRT Project"!

How to use "Project SQRT"

  1. Go to "SQRT Project" and find "Enter the number pole"
  2. Enter non-negative real number in "Enter the number pole"
  3. Press "Get SQRT" button the answer will appear lower
  4. That's it!

You can also clear data by using "Clear" button
